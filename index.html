About DAAN Blessings Trust

DAAN Blessings Trust is a charitable organization founded in 2026 by Mohammad Akram Khan. The Trust is dedicated to creating positive social change through education, healthcare, social welfare, skill development, and community empowerment initiatives.

Our mission is to uplift underprivileged communities by providing access to quality education, healthcare support, vocational training, and opportunities for sustainable growth.
